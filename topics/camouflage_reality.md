# Camouflage Reality

Seth's term for the physical universe — understood not as ultimate reality but as a "camouflage" or projection overlaid upon deeper, non-physical reality. The physical world is real but not primary.

## Core Definition
> **Early Sessions, Session 101:** "Your physical universe is a camouflage — a brilliant, intricate, and purposeful projection of inner reality into the medium of space and time. It is not an illusion in the sense of being false. It is a very specific, very coherent translation of inner experience into physical terms."

## Properties
- Physical reality is a valid system of experience, not an illusion to escape
- It is, however, a secondary reality — a projection of the primary inner universe
- The "camouflage" masks the true nature of space, time, matter, and causation
- Physical laws are the rules of the camouflage system, not laws of ultimate reality
- Beneath the camouflage, consciousness units (CUs) are the actual substrate
- Science studies the camouflage and develops accurate descriptions of it — but mistakes the camouflage for the whole
- Death does not end consciousness — it lifts the camouflage, revealing the fuller reality
- The physical body is camouflage for the inner body (the dream body / energy body)
- Emotions, thoughts, and beliefs are the language through which inner reality projects into camouflage form
- Coordinate points are places where the camouflage is "thinner" — where inner reality presses more directly through
