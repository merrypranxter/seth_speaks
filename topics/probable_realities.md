# Probable Realities

Seth's model of simultaneously existing alternate realities, each as real and valid as the physical world, arising from every decision point and moment of consciousness.

## Core Definition
> **Seth Speaks, Session 565:** "There are probable selves as legitimate as the self you know. In other probable realities, other you's are experiencing what you did not choose to experience here. All of these realities are equally real. None is more valid, more primary, or more 'true' than another."

## Properties
- All probabilities exist simultaneously — not in sequence but in parallel
- Consciousness explores probable realities via the focus of attention and belief
- Every significant decision point spawns new probable branches
- Probable selves share the same entity-source but differ in experience
- Communication between probable selves occurs through the deeper layers of the psyche
- Dreams often access probable realities directly
- The present moment is the pivot point from which probabilities fan outward
- Physical reality is simply the probability you are currently focused upon
- No probable reality is "wrong" — all serve the learning and expansion of the greater entity
