# Frameworks 1 & 2

Seth's model of inner and outer reality as two interpenetrating "frameworks" — Framework 1 being the visible physical world, and Framework 2 being the invisible creative source from which physical events emerge.

## Core Definition
> **The "Unknown" Reality Vol 2, Session 729:** "Framework 1 is the world as you know it — the arena of physical events, time, space, cause and effect. Framework 2 is the vast invisible creative source from which all Framework 1 events emerge. Everything that appears in Framework 1 is first formed in Framework 2."

## Framework 1: The Visible World
- The physical universe as perceived through the physical senses
- Operates by the laws of space, time, and causality as you know them
- Events appear sequential, causal, bounded by physical laws
- The arena of conscious, waking experience
- What science studies and describes

## Framework 2: The Creative Source
- The non-physical realm of inner reality, imagination, and creative potential
- Exists outside of physical time — simultaneous, not sequential
- Contains the templates for all physical events before they manifest
- Accessible through meditation, dreams, imagination, and focused inner attention
- The source of inspiration, healing, creativity, and "miraculous" events
- All probable realities exist within Framework 2
- Your beliefs, emotions, and intentions work primarily through Framework 2 to create Framework 1 events
- "Luck," synchronicity, and sudden shifts of circumstance are Framework 2 breaking through into Framework 1

## Using the Frameworks
- Direct your conscious intentions toward Framework 2, not only Framework 1
- Trust that Framework 2 will handle the logistics of manifestation
- Do not micromanage the path — focus on the desired end state and allow Framework 2 to organize events
- Health crises, when understood through the Framework model, reveal the Framework 2 beliefs driving Framework 1 symptoms
