# Reincarnation System

Seth's model of reincarnation — fundamentally non-linear, simultaneous, and driven by the expansion of consciousness rather than karmic debt or punishment.

## Core Definition
> **Seth Speaks, Session 537:** "You do not have a series of lives strung out like beads on a string, each one following the last in your terms of time. All of your lives are lived simultaneously. The self you call 'past life' is alive now, in its own present. You share the same entity, the same greater identity, but you are not the same personality."

## Properties
- Reincarnation is simultaneous, not sequential — all lives exist at once in the greater spacious present
- What appears as "past lives" are actually parallel lives from the perspective of the entity
- There is no karma as punishment or debt — only the drive of consciousness to expand and experience
- The entity chooses particular lives, genders, challenges, and gifts before birth
- Between physical lives, consciousness exists in non-physical reality, integrating experience
- Memory of other lives is accessible through hypnosis, dreams, and expanded states
- Déjà vu and strong inexplicable attractions/aversions are bleed-throughs from other simultaneous lives
- The purpose of physical reincarnation is the enrichment of the entity through the unique perspective of individual experience
- Reincarnation is one mode of consciousness expansion — not the only one; entities may also evolve through non-physical means
