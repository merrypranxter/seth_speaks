# Dream Reality

Seth's teaching that the dream state is not a lesser or illusory form of experience, but a fully valid, coherent reality system with its own geography, rules, and inhabitants — as real as the waking physical world.

## Core Definition
> **Seth, Dreams and Projections of Consciousness:** "The dream state is not a shadow of waking life. It is an alternate mode of consciousness with its own legitimacy, its own coherence, its own capacity for learning and experience. In the dream state you are no less real, no less yourself, than in the waking state."

## Properties
- The dream state constitutes a genuine reality system, not a simulation of waking life
- The dream body is as real to the dreamer as the physical body is to the waking self
- Consciousness naturally projects into the dream state every night as a form of maintenance and expansion
- Dreams access probable realities — you often experience, in dream form, events from probable selves
- Precognitive dreams are real communications from the probable future
- Deceased individuals can be encountered in dream reality — they exist there as genuinely as they ever did in physical life
- Deliberate out-of-body projection is an extension of the natural dreaming process
- The inner senses, which cannot be used in waking life, operate freely in the dream state
- Seth communicates with Jane partly through the dream state even outside of formal sessions

## Techniques for Dream Exploration
1. **Pre-sleep suggestion** — intend before sleep to remember and be aware in dreams
2. **Hypnagogic state cultivation** — extend awareness through the threshold between waking and sleep
3. **Dream recording** — keep a dream journal to reinforce recall and signal the unconscious of interest
4. **Deliberate projection intent** — after recording sufficient dreams, set the intent to consciously project
