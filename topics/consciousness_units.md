# Consciousness Units (CUs)

Seth's fundamental building blocks of all existence — the irreducible units of consciousness from which all physical and non-physical reality is constructed.

## Core Definition
> **Seth Speaks, Session 511:** "There is one source, and from that source pours a constant stream of what I will call consciousness units — the smallest, most indestructible elements of conscious awareness, which form the basis of all that is."

## Properties
- Indestructible — cannot be annihilated, only transformed
- Self-aware at their own level, however minute
- Carry coded information about the whole system (holographic in nature)
- Exist outside of physical time — they "flash" in and out of the physical system
- Respond to emotional intensity, expectation, and focused intention
- Form into EE (electromagnetic energy) units as they approach physical materialization
- Cluster according to similarity of intent and belief
- Constitute the fabric of both physical and non-physical reality
- Each CU contains, in coded form, the knowledge of the entire universe

## Relationship to EE Units
Consciousness Units are the fundamental substrate; **Electromagnetic Energy Units (EE units)** are what CUs become when they approach the threshold of physical materialization. The progression is: CU → EE Unit → Particle → Atom → Matter.
