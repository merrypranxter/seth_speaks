# Seth's Cosmological Model: A Map of Reality

A synthesis of Seth's multi-layered model of existence, from the most fundamental consciousness units to the outermost layers of physical experience.

---

## The Basic Cosmology

Seth's cosmology begins with a single premise: **consciousness is the primary substance of the universe**. Matter, energy, space, and time are all secondary — projections or translations of consciousness into specific experiential frameworks.

The model flows from the most fundamental to the most derivative:

```
ALL THAT IS (The Source)
       ↓
Consciousness Units (CUs)
       ↓
Electromagnetic Energy Units (EE Units)
       ↓
Particles / Subatomic Matter
       ↓
Atoms / Molecules
       ↓
Physical Objects / Events
       ↓
The Camouflage Reality (Physical Universe)
```

Each layer is real within its own terms, but each is a translation of the layer above it. Physical matter is real — but it is a specific, constrained expression of the more fundamental reality of consciousness.

---

## Layers of Reality

### Layer 1: All That Is
The ultimate source — beyond personality, beyond division, beyond description. Seth uses "All That Is" rather than "God" to avoid the anthropomorphic limitations of the God concept. All That Is is not separate from its creations; it experiences itself through every consciousness that exists.

> "All That Is is in a state of constant creative becoming. It does not sit outside its creation watching — it is its creation, experiencing itself from infinite simultaneous perspectives."

### Layer 2: Consciousness Units (CUs)
The fundamental "atoms" of consciousness. Indestructible, self-aware at their own level, holographic (each containing the whole in coded form). CUs are the actual fabric of reality — what matter "is" when you understand it completely.

→ [Full Topic: Consciousness Units](../topics/consciousness_units.md)

### Layer 3: EE Units (Electromagnetic Energy Units)
CUs as they approach physical materialization — they take on electromagnetic characteristics and become responsive to the specific parameters of the physical system (time, space, causality). EE units are the bridge between inner and outer reality.

### Layer 4: Particles and Matter
EE units manifesting as the particles described by physics. At this level, the camouflage system of physical reality begins. Physical laws operate here, but they are the rules of the camouflage, not ultimate reality.

### Layer 5: The Camouflage Reality
The physical universe as experienced by focused physical consciousness. Real within its own framework, purposeful, but not the totality of existence.

→ [Full Topic: Camouflage Reality](../topics/camouflage_reality.md)

## Consciousness Hierarchy

Seth describes a nested hierarchy of consciousness, from the smallest unit to the largest:

| Level | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Consciousness Units | Fundamental — the smallest aware unit |
| Cells | Organized CUs with collective awareness and intent |
| Organs | Collective consciousness of organized cells |
| The Body | Collective consciousness of all organs — serves the incarnating self |
| The Waking Ego | The focused, physically-oriented self |
| The Inner Self | The larger self that manages incarnation, dreams, and reality creation |
| The Entity (Soul) | The full soul-identity across all incarnations |
| Groups of Entities | Families of consciousness (Sumari, Speakers, etc.) |
| All That Is | The totality, experiencing itself through all levels simultaneously |

## Time and Probability Model

Seth's model of time is radical: **time as experienced physically is a construct of the camouflage system**. From the perspective of deeper reality:

- Past, present, and future all exist simultaneously
- Every moment of decision spawns probable realities
- The "present" is the only point of power — the only point from which inner reality can be directed
- Reincarnation is not sequential but simultaneous — "past lives" are concurrent lives

```
         PROBABLE REALITY A
        /
PRESENT → PROBABLE REALITY B (physical focus)
        \
         PROBABLE REALITY C
```

All probable realities are equally real. The focused physical consciousness simply experiences them as sequential choices. From Framework 2 (the inner creative source), all probabilities exist at once.
