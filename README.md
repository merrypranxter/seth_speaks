## Seth 101

Seth describes himself as "an energy personality essence no longer focused in physical reality." His core teaching: **you create your own reality** through beliefs, emotions, and imagination. Physical reality is a "camouflage" over a deeper multidimensional existence. Consciousness is the fundamental substance of the universe.
