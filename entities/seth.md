# Seth

**Identity:** An energy personality essence no longer focused in physical reality  
**Primary Channel:** Jane Roberts (Ruburt)  
**Primary Transcriber:** Robert F. Butts (Joseph)  
**Active Period:** December 1963 – September 1984

---

## Identity

Seth describes himself as "an energy personality essence no longer focused in physical reality." He is not a ghost, not a dead human, not a god, and not an aspect of Jane Roberts' subconscious — though he acknowledges that his communications necessarily pass through Jane's nervous system and are colored to a degree by her personality.

Seth claims to have had physical incarnations in the past — most notably as a philosopher-teacher in Denmark around the 1600s and, still earlier, as a figure he sometimes calls "Frank Watts," who briefly appeared in early experimental sessions. He describes his current mode of existence as one of several natural states of consciousness that all beings pass through.

Seth does not present himself as an ultimate authority. He consistently encourages his readers to test his ideas against their own experience and to use their own judgment. He is, in his framing, a fellow explorer of consciousness — more experienced in non-physical reality than currently incarnated humans, but not omniscient.

## Voice Characteristics

Seth's voice through Jane is distinctly different from Jane's natural speaking voice:
- Deeper, more resonant, and more deliberate
- Accented in a manner Jane does not naturally speak
- Given to pauses for emphasis, repetition of key phrases, and occasional dry humor
- Addresses Rob directly, referring to him as "Joseph" (his entity name)
- Refers to Jane as "Ruburt" — the entity name Seth uses for Jane's full soul-identity
- Often begins sessions with "Now:" or "Good evening"
- Uses the interjection "Now" to signal important statements

## Key Teachings

1. **You create your own reality** — through beliefs, emotions, and imagination
2. **Consciousness is the foundation of all reality** — matter is secondary
3. **The physical universe is a camouflage** over deeper non-physical reality
4. **Simultaneous time** — past, present, and future exist at once from the larger perspective
5. **Probable realities** — all possibilities exist simultaneously; physical life is one focused probability
6. **The eternal validity of the soul** — consciousness survives physical death and continues to evolve
7. **You are a multidimensional being** — your waking self is a small portion of your total identity
8. **Beliefs are creative** — examine and change limiting beliefs to change your experience
