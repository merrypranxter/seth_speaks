# Robert F. Butts (Joseph)

**Full Name:** Robert F. Butts  
**Entity Name:** Joseph (Seth's name for Rob's full soul-identity)  
**Born:** June 20, 1919 — Watertown, New York  
**Died:** May 26, 2008 — Elmira, New York  
**Role:** Artist / Session Transcriber / Editor

---

## Background

Robert F. Butts was an American artist and illustrator who became the primary transcriber and editor of the Seth sessions. He studied art formally and worked as a commercial illustrator throughout much of his life, while also pursuing fine art painting.

Rob met Jane Roberts in the late 1950s. They never married but shared their lives from that point onward, conducting the Seth sessions together from their apartment in Elmira, New York. While Jane was the channel, Rob was in every sense an equal partner in the creation of the Seth Material — his role as questioner, skeptic, recorder, and eventually editor shaped the corpus as profoundly as Jane's channeling.

Rob's own entity name, Joseph, occasionally appears in the sessions when Seth addresses him directly or discusses his past lives.

## Role as Transcriber

Rob's contribution to the Seth Material cannot be overstated. Without his meticulous transcription work, the sessions would not exist in the form they do:

- **Stenography:** Rob developed a rapid shorthand system to capture Seth's words in real time, including gestures, pauses, and tonal emphasis
- **Questioner:** Rob asked clarifying questions during sessions, steering Seth toward elaboration on important points
- **Skeptic in residence:** Rob's ongoing skepticism — he never fully "believed" in a credulous way — provided a grounding influence on the material
- **Typist:** After sessions, Rob typed up his shorthand notes, creating the formal session transcripts
- **Editor:** Rob edited all the published Seth books, wrote introductions and notes, and oversaw posthumous publications
- **Archivist:** Rob maintained the complete archive of session transcripts, notes, and related materials

## Notes Style

Rob's annotations within published sessions are visually distinct — typically in parentheses and italics in the published books. They include:
- His own questions and interjections during sessions (shown as: *"Can you elaborate on that?"*)
- Descriptions of Jane's physical state, trance depth, and gestures
- Contextual notes about what Jane and Rob had been doing before the session
- Cross-references to earlier sessions
- Post-session observations about the material
- Descriptions of unusual phenomena that occurred during sessions

Rob's notes are an invaluable part of the corpus — they provide grounding, context, and a human counterpoint to Seth's often abstract cosmological teachings.

## Life After Jane

After Jane's death in 1984, Rob dedicated the remaining decades of his life to archiving, editing, and publishing the unpublished Seth material. He edited and saw to publication:
- *Dreams, Evolution, and Value Fulfillment* (1986)
- *The Magical Approach* (1995)
- *The Way Toward Health* (1997)
- *The Early Sessions* Books 1-9 (1997-2003)
- *The Personal Sessions* Books 1-7

Rob died on May 26, 2008, in Elmira, New York.
