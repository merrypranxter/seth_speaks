# Seth, Dreams and Projections of Consciousness

**Published:** 1987 (posthumously)  
**Sessions:** Various (drawn primarily from Early Sessions and supplementary material)  
**Core Themes:** Out-of-body experience, dream reality as valid reality system, projection techniques, the dream body, consciousness expansion

## Overview

This volume, compiled and edited by Jane Roberts with Robert Butts, focuses on Seth's extensive teachings about the dream state and out-of-body projection. It draws from sessions across the broader Seth corpus and includes Jane's own accounts of her out-of-body experiences, which preceded and accompanied the Seth sessions.

The book stands as the most comprehensive single-volume treatment of Seth's view that the dream state is a legitimate reality system — not a lesser or illusory experience, but a full-spectrum alternate mode of consciousness with its own coherence, geography, and rules.
