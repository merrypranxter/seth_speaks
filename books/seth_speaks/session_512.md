# Session 512: More on Consciousness Units
**Book:** Seth Speaks  
**Date:** January 22, 1970  
**Topics:** `#consciousness-units` `#ee-units` `#reality-creation`

---

## Jane's Notes (Pre-session)
Rob and I had been reviewing the previous session's notes about consciousness units before Seth came through tonight. There was a strong sense of continuity from the previous evening — as though Seth had been waiting to pick up exactly where he left off.

## Session Begins

Now: Good evening.

("Good evening, Seth.")

I told you previously that consciousness units form the basis of all physical experience. Tonight I would like to expand upon this, for it is crucial that Ruburt and Joseph understand the mechanism by which thought becomes thing — how the interior universe projects itself outward into the camouflage reality you call physical life.

Each consciousness unit is, in your terms, indestructible. It cannot be annihilated, only transformed. It carries within it the full intent of the entity from which it springs, and in this way the universe is self-aware at every level of its existence.

("Can you say more about how EE units relate to consciousness units?")

Electromagnetic energy units — EE units — are the form that consciousness units take when they approach the threshold of physical materialization. They are, if you will, the bridge between inner and outer reality. They carry emotional charge, they respond to expectation, and they are drawn into clusters by the intensity of belief and desire.

Your physical body is a brilliant construction of these units, organized by the inner self according to patterns set down before birth, yet continuously renewed in each moment of your experience. The cells of your body are conscious. They contain their own awareness, their own intent, their own communication with the greater body of which they are a part.

[The session continued for approximately ninety minutes, with Seth elaborating on the hierarchical nature of consciousness units, their relationship to time, and their role in dream creation.]

## Key Passages
- "A single consciousness unit contains, in coded form, the knowledge of the entire universe — much as a seed contains the blueprint of the tree."
- "EE units do not exist in your time. They flash in and out of your physical system, appearing and disappearing at a rate your instruments cannot yet measure."
- "The coordination of these units into physical form is the creative act of the inner self, performed millions of times each second."
