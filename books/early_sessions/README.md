# The Early Sessions: Books 1-9

**Published:** 1997–2003 (originally recorded 1963–1969)  
**Sessions:** 1-510  
**Core Themes:** Foundations of the Seth Material, ESP experiments, initial cosmology, reincarnation, the nature of time and consciousness

## Overview

The Early Sessions represent the beginning of the Seth Material — the first decade of Jane Roberts' channeling work with Seth. These sessions preceded Seth's major books and contain the raw foundations of his cosmological framework, as well as extensive personal guidance for Jane and Rob and experiments in extrasensory perception.

The Early Sessions were published posthumously in nine volumes, covering the full range of Seth's initial teachings.
